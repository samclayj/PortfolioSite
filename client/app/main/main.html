About Me

Hi, I'm Sam

I've been interested in computers web design since I was around 13 years old. I was homeschooled from a young age and given the opportunity to explore a lot of my specific interests. I began web design at age 13, and eventually moved on to iOS development a few years later. My interest in technology continued, and I'm now double majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics at Stephen F. Austin State University.


I grew up traveling back and forth between Houston and Lake Sam Rayburn almost weekly. Some of my favorite pass times include running and hammocking while reading a book. Lake Sam Rayburn was an incredible place to grow up. I was free to wander around the woods with my friends and I will never forget all of the long walks on the shore building imaginary kingdoms and fortresses.



I love learning about new technologies. Currently, I'm fascinated by fullstack web development, especially using the MEAN stack to create interactive web applications. New tech is definitely a hobby of mine, usually resulting in me purchasing (and sometimes returning) new gadgets and toys as soon as they come out, and my bank account allows for it.


I've had some amazing opportunities extended to me at Stephen F. Austin, and I continue to enjoy my college experience every day (despite the all night study sessions). The Computer Science program at this university has provided me with a challenging and rewarding experience. Every professor I've encountered has been compassionate and shown an incredible interest in my success as a student and a professional.


My father went to school at SFA, and I grew up involved in many of the traditions and events at the university. I'm thrilled that I have gotten to take part in this experience as a student.


I am so thankful for the time I have spent at SFA. I know that the relationships I have made here and the knowledge I have gained will benefit me and bring my joy for years to come.


In addition to my education, I've had the incredible opportunity to pursue two internships with GE Oil & Gas in their IT Leadership program. These internships provided me with a wonderful experience in cyber security, project management, and full-stack development. Additionally, communicating and coordinating with so many diverse teams was amazing. In my last internship, my teammates and I won the intern innovation contest for the summer. I'm extremely proud to have been a part of this team.


The projects I was able to take part in and the individuals I met provided me with an amazing opportunity to experience IT and software development in the real-world. I feel enormously more comfortable interacting with people and teams in a business environment, and more confident that the skills I have been cultivating can actually contribute to an organization in a meaningful way.


In the future, my goal is to continue gaining as much experience as possible in software development. I hope my experience and my education in mathematics will allow me to take on more technical roles, and that I can become an integral part of a team. I also plan on furthering my education, attaining my graduate degree and doctorate in Computer Science in the future.
