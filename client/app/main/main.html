Hi, I'm Sam

I've been interested in computers web design since I was around 13 years old. I was homeschooled from a young age and given the opportunity to explore a lot of my specific interests. I began web design at age 13, and eventually moved on to iOS development a few years later. My interest in technology continued, and I'm now double majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics at Stephen F. Austin State University.


I grew up traveling back and forth between Houston and Lake Sam Rayburn almost weekly. Some of my favorite pass times include running and hammocking while reading a book.


I love learning about new technologies. Currently, I'm fascinated by fullstack web development, especially using the MEAN stack and how it will change web applications.
